<ModAsset location="oritech:hand_drill" /> The hand drill is a rechargeable and unbreakable tool that functions as a pickaxe and shovel.
It can also be enchanted. To charge the hand drill, place it in an [energy storage](../logistics/energy) block or wear an [exo chestplate](../tools/exo_armor).
